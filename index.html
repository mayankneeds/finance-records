Finance Records

Overview

Finance Records is a lightweight, privacy-focused Android application designed to help you track your daily expenses and manage your personal budget. Your data stays on your device—we don't track you or sell your information.

Key Features

  • Local-First Tracking: All your financial data is stored securely on your phone using a local database.
  • Categorized Spending: Organize your costs into custom categories like Food, Utilities, Transport, and more.
  • Simple Visualization: Get a clear picture of where your money goes with intuitive summaries.
  • Secure Backups: (Optional) Sync your data to your private Google Drive for peace of mind.

Download for Android

The safest way to install the app is through the official Google Play Store.

Get Finance Records on Google Play ➔

Privacy & Transparency

  • No Accounts Required: You don’t need to sign up or provide an email to use the app.
  • Data Control: You can export or delete your data at any time from within the app settings.
  • Permissions: We only ask for the minimum permissions required for basic functionality (like Storage for exports).
