Privacy Policy: Finance Records

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

This document describes how the Finance Records Android application (the "App") handles user information.

1. Local-Only Data Storage

The App is built as a self-contained utility.

  • Financial Data: All entries, categories, and logs are stored exclusively in a secure database on your own device.
  • Developer Access: I (the developer) have zero access to your data. There is no central server, and no data is transmitted to me at any time.

2. No Account Required

The App does not require a login, username, or email. Your data is tied to your physical device, not an online account.

3. Google Drive Sync (User Controlled)

If you enable the optional backup feature, the App interacts only with your personal Google Drive storage. No data is shared with the developer or any third party during this process.

4. Limited Usage Tracking (Google Services)

To help keep the app stable, it uses standard Google libraries:

  • Crashlytics: Sends anonymous technical reports if the app crashes.
  • Analytics: Provides basic stats on total active users (standard for all Play Store apps).

5. Disclaimer & Responsibility

  • Data Safety: Because the data is stored locally, the user is responsible for managing their own backups via the Google Drive feature.
  • Device Security: The App provides an optional fingerprint lock; however, the overall security of the records depends on the user’s device security (pin/password/biometrics).
  • As-Is Basis: The App is provided as a productivity tool "as-is" without any warranties. The developer is not responsible for any financial decisions or data loss occurring due to hardware failure.
