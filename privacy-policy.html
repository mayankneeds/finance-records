Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At Finance Records, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection, use, and disclosure practices. By using the Finance Records application ("the App"), you agree to the policies outlined below.

1. Data Collection & Privacy First

Finance Records is built on a Privacy First architecture. We do not require any sign-up or login. All your financial data, including transactions, accounts, and budgets, are stored locally on your device.

  • Local Storage: Your data stays on your phone. We do not have servers that collect, store, or process your financial entries.
  • iCloud Sync: If you choose to enable iCloud synchronization across your Apple devices, your data is securely synced via your personal Apple ID using Apple's CloudKit. We do not have access to this data.

2. Third-Party Services

The App may include embedded tools or APIs from third parties (e.g., Google APIs, crash reporting). These services may collect anonymous usage data or device information depending on their respective privacy policies.

3. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the effective date at the top of this document or via in-app notifications.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to our support channel.
